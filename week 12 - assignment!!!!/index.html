What is a bonsai kitten?

You can finally have a perfect cat with Bonsai Kitten! Bonsai Kittens are kittens raised in a jar so it bones mold to the shape of the jar as it grows, just like a bonsai plant! Just simply place your kitten in your desired shape jar and leave it to grow. Simple as that! You will no longer need to worry about your cat running away or attacking you, you can watch it grow as its adorable face and paws will squish up against the jar! There is no need to remove the kitten for feeding or cleaning. It is perfectly safe and hygienic for any breed and size kitten to grow in. Discovered by Dr Michael Wong Chang and approved by animal right and welfare organizations, you no longer have to have a normal shaped cat! Let your cat be unique and they will forever love you! Try bonsai kitten today!

Method

Having a Bonsai Kitten is not as hard as it seems!

• Simply place your under 3 month kitten in one of our specially designed jars
• Blend up your kittens food and push it through the tube provided into your kittens mouth 3 times a day
• Watch it’s little face and paws push up against the jar
• Release when the cat is two years of age

Our jars are designed so that the kittens can eat safely without being removed from its jar. It is also hygienic with a small hole so urine and feces can pass through the container. With a survival rate of 90% you are guaranteed to have a good laugh and some cute aww’s
